A happy cat is a healthy cat. Look for these reassuring signs that your feline friend
is living their best life.

## 1. Purring in relaxed settings

Purring during cuddles or naps is the classic contentment signal.

## 2. Kneading ("making biscuits")

This kittenhood behavior carries into adulthood as a sign of comfort and security.

## 3. A relaxed, upright tail

A tail held high with a gentle curl is the feline equivalent of a smile.

## 4. Good appetite

Steady, healthy eating habits usually indicate a comfortable, stress-free cat.

## 5. Playfulness

Chasing toys and "hunting" games show mental and physical wellbeing.

<Callout type="success" title="Bonus sign">
If your cat exposes their belly, they feel safe — it's one of the most vulnerable
positions a cat can take.
</Callout>

## 6–10: The rest of the checklist

6. Grooming themselves regularly.
7. Sleeping soundly in open areas.
8. Greeting you at the door.
9. Slow-blinking at you.
10. Gentle, chirpy vocalizations.

Spot most of these? Congratulations — you're doing a wonderful job. 🐱
